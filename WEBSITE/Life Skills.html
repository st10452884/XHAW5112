Course Overview:

Empower yourself with essential skills for everyday life through our comprehensive Life Skills Course. Designed to provide practical knowledge and build confidence, this course covers vital topics that help you navigate personal and professional situations effectively. It's perfect for anyone seeking to improve their basic literacy, financial skills, and understanding of legal rights.

Course Content:

  1. Opening a Bank Account
    • Learn the steps involved in opening and managing a bank account.
    • Understand different types of bank accounts (savings, credit, etc.) and their benefits.
    • Get familiar with basic banking terms, fees, and how to use ATMs and online banking services.
  2. Basic Labour Law (Know Your Rights)
    • Learn about minimum wage regulations, working hours, leave entitlements, and fair treatment at the workplace.
    • Gain a fundamental understanding of labour laws and worker rights.
    • Understand the importance of employment contracts and what to look for when signing one.
  3. Basic Reading and Writing Literacy
    • Improve your reading comprehension and writing skills to better understand written information.
    • Practice writing simple documents such as letters, emails, and forms.
    • Gain confidence in using literacy skills for daily tasks, including reading signs, instructions, and documents.
  4. Basic Numeric Literacy
    • Develop skills in basic arithmetic, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
    • Learn how to budget, manage expenses, and handle money wisely.
    • Understand how to read and interpret simple financial documents, such as bills, receipts, and bank statements.
