Course Overview:

With this practical sewing course, discover how to sew and unlock your creativity! This course offers step-by-step guidance on a variety of sewing techniques and is intended for both begginers and experienced seamstresses.You will be confident in your ability to design and alter clothing by the end of the course.

Course Content:

  1. Types of Stiches
    • Learn essential hand and machine stitches, including straight stich, zigzag stitch, and backstitch.
    • Understand when to use different stitches for various fabrics and sewing tasks.
    • Master decorative stitches to add unique touches to your projects.
  2. Threading a Sewing Machine
    • Learn how to set up and thread a sewing machine correctly.
    • Understandhow to wind and install the bobbin
    • Get comfortable with adjusting stitch length, tension, and selecting appropriate needles for different fabrics.
  3. Sewing Buttons
    • Discover how to sew buttons by hand and with a sewing machine.
    • Learn to attach different types of buttons, including flat and shank buttons.
    • Understand the techniques for creating buttonholes using a sewing machine.
  4. Zips
    • Learn the steps for sewing zippers into garments, including centered, lapped, and invisible zips.
    • Understand the use of different zipper feet for your sewing machine.
    • Practice finishing techniques to ensure a professional look.
  5. Hems and Seams
    • Master basic hemming techniques, such as blind hem, double-fold hem, and rolled hem.
    • Learn various seam types, includong Freanch seam, flat-felled seam, and overlocked seam
    • Understand how to finish raw edges to prevent fraying.
  6. Alterations
    • Gain skils in taking measurements and making basic alterations to garments.
    • Learn how to shorten or lengthen hems, take in or let out seams, and adjust waistbands.
    • Understand how to repair damaged clothing, such as fixing goles or replacing zippers.
  7. Designing and Sewing New Garments
    • Explore the process of designing and drafting patterns for custom garments.
    • Learn how to choose fabrics and prepare them for sewing.
    • Practice sewing complete garments, such as skirts, tops, or simple dresses.
