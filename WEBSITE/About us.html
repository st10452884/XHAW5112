About Us

Our Mission

At Empowering the Nation, we are dedicated to providing essential skills training for domestic workers and gardeners to help them achieve better job opportunities, higher wages, and the chance to start their own business. We believe that by equipping individuals with valuable skills, we are building stronger communities and creating pathways to success for those who need it most.

Our Story

Empowering the Nation was founded in 2018 by Precious Radebe, a passionate advocate for the upliftment and education of domestic workers and gardeners. Inspired by her own family's experience-her parents and elderly relatives never had the opportunity to pursue formal eduaction or skills traininf-Precious established this initiative to help others from her community break free from that cycle.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. Together, we can create a brighter future for all.
