Course Overview:

Learn the skills needed to keep a garden thriving all year round with our practical Garden Maintenace Course. tjis course provides comprehensive training in maintaining and caring for a variety of plants, from indigenous to exotic species. Gain valuable knowledge about proper watering, pruning, propagation, and planting techniques to ensure a healthy, beautiful garde.

Course Content:

  1. Water Restrictions and the Watering Requirements of Indigenous and Exotic Plans
    • Understanding Water Restrictions: Learn about local water usage regulations and how they affect gardening practices.
    • Watering Techniques: Discover efficient watering methods, such as drip irrigation and mulching, to conserve water while meeeting plant needs
    • Indigenous vs. Exotic Plants: Understand the different watering requirements of native(which may need less water) compared exotic species.
    • Drought-Resistant Gardening: Learn how to select and care for drought-tolerant plants to create a water-wise garden.
  2. Pruning and Propagation of Plants
    • Pruning Techniques: Master the proper methods for pruning trees, shrubs, and flowers to encourage healthy growth and flowering.
    • Seasonal Pruning: Learn when and how to prune different plant species for optimal results.
    • Propagation Methods: Discover various techniques for propagating plants, including seed planting, cuttings, layering, and division
    • Tools and Safety: Get familiar with tools used for pruning and propagation, and learn how to use the safely and effectively.
  3. Planting Techniques for Defferent Plant Types
    • Soil Preparations: Understand the importance of soil quality and how to prepare the soil for differnt types of plants.
    • Planting Trees and Shrubs: Learn the correct depth, spacing, and support needed when planting larger plants.
    • Transplanting Techniques: Discover how to transpalnt plants safely without causing damage to roost.
    • Container Gardening: Gain insights into growing plants in pots and containers, including selecting the right potting mix and providing adequate drainage.
  4. Preparation and Cooking Meals
    • Basic Cooking Techniques:Master essential cooking methods such as boiling, steaming, abking, frying, and grilling.
    • Kitchen Safety and Hygiene: Learn best practices for handling food, using kitchen tools, and avoiding cross-contamination
    • Recipe Adaptation: Discover how to modify recipes to suit different tastes and dietary needs.
    • Presentation Skills: Gain tips on plating and presenting dishes to make meals visually appealing.
