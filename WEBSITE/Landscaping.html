Course Overview:

Transform outdoor spaces into stunning gardens and landscapes with our practical Landscaping Course. Ideal for beginners and enthusiasts, this course provides a comprehensive guide to garden design and Landscaping techniques. Hain the skills needed to create beautiful, functional, and sustainable outdoor environments.

Course Content:

  1. Indigenous and Exotic Trees and Plants
    • Learn to identify a variety of indigenous and exotic plant species.
    • Understand the benefits and challenges of using native vs. non-native plants in landscaping.
    • Gain knowledge about plant characteristics, growth habits, and maintenance needs.
  2. Fixed Structures(Fountains, Statues, Benches, Tables, Built-in Braais)
    • Discover how to incorporate fixed structures like fountains, dtatues, and garden furniture into your design.
    • Learn the principles of placing and integrating built-in features, such as braais or fire pits, to enhance functionality.
    • Understand how to use hardscaping elements to create focal points and complement plant life.
  3. Balancing of Plants and Trees in a Garden
    • Master the art of balaning different plant heights, textures, and densities to create harmony.
    • Learn how to strategically place trees, shrubs, and flower for visual appeal and spatial balance.
    • Understand how to maintain ecological balance by considering water usage, soil conditions, and plant compability
  4. Aesthetics of Plant Shapes and Colours
    • Explore the impact of plant shapes, sizes, and colours on garden aesthetics.
    • Learn how to use colour theory to create visually pleasing and mood-enhancing garden designs.
    • Understand the role of seasonal changes in plant colour and shape, and how to plan for year-round beauty.
  5. Garden
    • Learn about different garden styles, such as formal, informal, Mediterranean, and contemporary.
    • Gain insights into creating a well-thought-out garden layout, including pathways, garden beds, and open spaces.
    • Understand how to draw up plans and sketches for garden projects, including scaling and site mapping.
