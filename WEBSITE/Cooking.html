Course Overview:

Our Cooking Courses is designed to help you create delicios, balanced meals that meet the nutritional needs of a healthy lifestyle. Whether you're a beginner in the kitchen or looking to expand your culinary skills, this course covers everything from understanding nutrition to planning and preparing meals. Gain confidence in cooking nutritious dishes that everyone will enjoy!

Course Content:

  1. Nutritional Requirements for a Healthy Body
    • Understanding Balanced Diets: Learn about the essential nutrients the body meeds, including vitamins, minerals, and fiber.
    • Dietary Guidelines: Discover how to meet daily nutritional requirements for various age groups.
    • Portion Control: Understand the importance of portion sizes in maintaining a healthy weight and preventing overeating.
    • Special Dietary Needs: Learn to accomodate dietary restrictions, such as vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or lactose-free.
  2. Types of Proteins, Carbohydrates, and Vegetables
    • Proteins: Explore different sources of protein, including animal-based and plany-based options, and their roles in muscle growth and repair.
    • Carbohydrates: Learn about simple and complex carbohydrates, their energy-providing roles, and how to choose healthier options.
    • Vegetables: Discover the nutritional benefits of various vegetables and how to incorporate them into meals for added vitamins and minerals.
    • Combining Food Groups: Understand how to create balanced meals by combining proteins, carbohydrates, and vegetables effectively.
  3. Planning Meals
    • Creating Meal Plans: Learn how to design weekly meal plans that include breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks
    • Budget-Friendly Meal Planning: Discover tips for planning nutritious meals while sticking to a budget.
    • Incorporating Variety: Ensure that your plans include a range of foods to provide different nutrients and prevent meal fatigue.
    • Using Seasonal Ingridients: Learn to plan meals using fresh, seasonal ingridients to maximize flavour and nutritional value.
  4. Preparation and Cooking Meals
    • Basic Cooking Techniques:Master essential cooking methods such as boiling, steaming, abking, frying, and grilling.
    • Kitchen Safety and Hygiene: Learn best practices for handling food, using kitchen tools, and avoiding cross-contamination
    • Recipe Adaptation: Discover how to modify recipes to suit different tastes and dietary needs.
    • Presentation Skills: Gain tips on plating and presenting dishes to make meals visually appealing.
